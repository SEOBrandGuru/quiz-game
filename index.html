Why these quizzes exist

Most buying decisions are made with incomplete information. Not because people are careless — but because the information that actually matters is buried in technical specifications, regulatory frameworks, and academic research that most people never encounter.

These quizzes extract the most practically important knowledge from each domain and test it directly. The goal is not to embarrass — it's to close the gap between what buyers know and what they'd want to know if they knew it existed.
